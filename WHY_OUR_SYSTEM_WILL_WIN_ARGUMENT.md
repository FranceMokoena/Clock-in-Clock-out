### ❌ **70% of Enterprise Software Projects Fail Due to Poor Adoption**

**Research Fact:** According to industry statistics, 70% of enterprise software projects fail not because of technical issues, but because users refuse to adopt them. [1]

**Why?**
1. **Steep Learning Curve**: Users need 40-80 hours of training
2. **Unintuitive Interface**: Can't find features, get lost
3. **Poor Error Handling**: Generic messages, no guidance
4. **Feature Overload**: Too many options, decision paralysis
5. **No Real-Time Feedback**: Users don't know if they're doing it right
